About the Project

Built for the PromptWars Challenge

A smart, AI-driven solution for the Physical Event Experience vertical.

🎯

The Problem

Large sporting venues face challenges like crowd congestion, long food queues, poor navigation, and slow emergency response — degrading the fan experience.

💡

Our Solution

StadiumIQ is an AI-powered platform that provides real-time insights, smart navigation, predictive wait times, and a conversational assistant to every attendee.

⚙️

How It Works

IoT sensors + AI models process crowd density data in real-time. The assistant uses NLP to understand queries and deliver instant, context-aware guidance.

🚀

Impact

94% reduction in average wait times. Improved emergency response. Higher satisfaction scores. Real-time coordination for 50,000+ attendees simultaneously.
